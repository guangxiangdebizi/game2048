## English Version

### 1. Overview

Welcome to "2048 - Classic Number Puzzle" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy aims to help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

**Important Statement: The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.**

### 2. Information Collection

The App is a completely offline single-player game. We **DO NOT collect** any of the following information:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Device information
- Location information
- Usage data
- Contacts or photos
- Any other personal data

### 3. Local Data Storage

The App only stores the following data locally on your device:
- **High Score**: Uses localStorage to store your game high score, saved only on your device
- **Game State**: May temporarily save current game progress (only while the app is running)

This data:
- ✅ Is stored only on your device
- ✅ Is not uploaded to any server
- ✅ Is not shared with any third parties
- ✅ Can be completely removed by uninstalling the app

### 4. Permissions

The App **DOES NOT request any device permissions**, including but not limited to:
- ❌ No network permission
- ❌ No location permission
- ❌ No camera permission
- ❌ No contacts permission
- ❌ No storage permission

### 5. Third-Party Services

The App **DOES NOT use any third-party services**, including:
- No advertising services
- No analytics tools
- No social media integration
- No cloud storage services

### 6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any information, there are no children's privacy concerns.

### 7. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any data, your privacy is completely secure. All game data is stored only on your device and is under your complete control.

### 8. Your Rights
